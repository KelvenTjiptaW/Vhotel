Our Facilities

Swimming Pool

Our swimming pool boasts an elegant and modern design. Surrounded by lush tropical gardens and shady trees, the pool creates a serene and rejuvenating atmosphere.

Gym

Our gym is equipped with the latest state-of-the-art fitness equipment from leading brands. From cardio machines such as treadmills, stationary bikes, and ellipticals to strength training equipment including free weights, weight machines, and resistance bands, you'll find a comprehensive selection to suit your fitness preferences.

Spa

Step into our serene spa sanctuary, carefully crafted to create a calming ambiance. Soft lighting, gentle music, and aromatic scents will transport you to a state of pure relaxation, allowing you to unwind and let go of tension.
